10 Bollywood Stars with Different Past

Top 10 Richest Indians(2016)

10 Most powerful Militaries

Summary:- They were not handed silver spoon of acting but made it to the industry on their own with their exceptional talent. Let's see some of the Bollywood stars who had their fair share of stints in other industries before joining Bollywood.
10. R Madhavan

Early Days:- Madhavan graduated with a degree in electronics.During his college years, Madhavan was actively involved in extra-curricular military training and regarded among the best NCC cadets and during this time took training with British Army, the Royal Navy he wanted to join Royal Air Force but missed the age cutoff.He began teaching courses in public speaking.
Movies:- Tanu Weds Manu,3 Idiots,Rehna Hai Tere Dil Mein.

9. John Abraham

Early Days:- John Abraham started his modelling career appearing in the music videos .Later on he joined and worked for different AD agencies .
Movies:- Dhoom,Madras Cafe,Dostana

8. Jacqueline Fernandez

Early Days:- Jacqueline used to host television shows in Bahrain when she was 14.She has a degree in mass communication.After graduating she worked as a television reporter in Sri Lanka and at the same time she pursued modelling and won Miss Universe Sri Lanka pageant in 2006.
Movies:- Housefull,Kick,Murder 2.

7. Parineeti Chopra

Early Days:- Parineeti wanted to become an investment banker during her early days.At the age of 17, she moved to London, where she received a triple honours degree in business, finance and economics from Manchester Business School.While studying, she also worked part-time for the Manchester United Football Club as the team leader of the catering department.Later on she became marketting Professional.
Movies:- Ladies vs Ricky Bahl,Ishaqzaade,Shuddh Desi Romance.

6. Akshay Kumar

Early Days:- He obtained his black belt in Taekwondo in India.After that he went to Bangkok, Thailand to learn Muay Thai while at the same time he worked as a chef and waiter.He came back to India to Teach Martial Arts.
Movies:- Khiladi,Singh Is Kinng,Hera Pheri

5. Sushant Singh Rajput

Early Days:- Sushant Singh was a very intelligent student he was a national Olympiad winner in Physics.In his 12th standard board exams, he secured AIR-7 in AIEEE (All-India Rank-7 in the All India Engineering Entrance Exam) and cleared many enterance exams. He was pursuing engineering in DTU but later droped out to become an actor.
Movies:- Kai Po Che,Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!,M. S. Dhoni: The Untold Story

4. Boman Irani

Early Days:- After his polytechnic diploma course, he joined the Taj Mahal Palace & Tower and worked there as a waiter and room service staff.Thereafter, he joined his mother to run their ancestral bakery shop.He began acting very late in his life.
Movies:- Munnabhai M.B.B.S.,Khosla Ka Ghosla,Ferrari Ki Sawaari

3. Nawazuddin Siddiqui

Early Days:- He has graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Chemistry.Following this, he worked as a chemist in Vadodara for a year and has also worked as a Guard.
Movies:- Gangs of Wasseypur,Badlapur,Manjhi - The Mountain Man

2. Mehmood Ali

Early Days:- Mehmood did many odd jobs before getting into mainline acting like working as a table tennis teacher,as a driver -to name a few.
Movies:- Bhoot Bangla,Padosan,Bombay to Goa

1. Shivaji Rao Gaekwad(Rajnikanth)

Early Days:- After completing his school education ,Rajnikanth did many jobs including that of a coolie,carpenter and later on became a bus conductor at the same time he took part in several stage plays.
Movies:- Phool Bane Angaray,Sivaji - The Boss,Robot.
