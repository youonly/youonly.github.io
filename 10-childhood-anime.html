10. Kochikame

10.Kochikame

Kochikame ,is a Japanese comedy manga series written and illustrated by Osamu Akimoto and revolves around the misadventures of middle-aged cop Kankichi Ryotsu.

Since its inception on September 1976 it has over 1800 chapters collected into 199 tankobon volumes, making it the manga series with the highest number of volumes. The manga has been adapted into a television anime series two theatrical animated films, two live-action movies, several stage adaptations, and a live-action television series.

TV Asahi named the anime number 36 on its list of the Top 100 Anime.
