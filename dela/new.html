Our Mission

 

The genetic code is the common thread that bind all living things, governing how they function. Genetic Intelligence harnesses this common power to help people live healthier lives with better medicine and sustainable food. To accomplish this goal, we developed transformative genetic machine learning compass that far overcomes the limits of traditional GWAS-based approaches to gene discovery

Precision medicine

Our technology helps unlock the full promise of genetic therapeutic modalities (e.g., CRISPR) and NGS-based diagnostics thanks to an accurate and rapid phenotype-genotype match.

Put another way, human society currently has many leaks (i.e., bad genes in sick patients) and great plumbers (i.e., novel RNA and DNA-repairing systems), but we don't know where to send the plumbers to patch the leaks in the vast majority of cases (>95%). Genetic Intelligence is filling this gap.



Metabolic engineering

Metabolic Engineering (the rewiring of cellular metabolism and interacting pathways to biosynthesize large and small molecules) is currently limited by lack of knowledge of the genes and pathways that are responsible for complex functions.

Genetic Intelligence’s phenotype-genotype match technology enables the engineering of better host strains to achieve greater titers in your fermentation processes with less feed resources.



Agriculture

Rising global population and shifting consumer tastes have brought challenging opportunities for agricultural companies.

We help deliver greater crop productivity with concomitant maintenance of genetic diversity. We achieve this outcome through the elucidation of the complex genes and pathways that regulate phenotype including: product yield, growth speed, temperature resistance, pest resistance, and pigmentation.



Animal Health

Genetic Intelligence believes in bringing the tremendous benefits of next generation therapeutics to improve the welfare of our companion animals but also those of livestock.

For livestock and aquaculture, rising demand for food has forced protein-producing populations into increasing genetic uniformity, with risky implications for biodiversity. Genetic Intelligence has the technology to help stakeholders reverse these trends. We are committed to help producers maintain genetic variability while still meeting the challenge of high production quality and yield.
